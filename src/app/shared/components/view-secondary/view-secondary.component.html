How to use fwd_Story
info Reflect

Think about the work you've been doing, need to do, and want to do. Use this guide to help.

info Write

Add/update your priorities, accomplishments, and aspirations.

info Align

Share and discuss with your Career Adviser, project lead, or other work partners.

info Revisit

Continue to update your fwd_Story, and use it to have meaningful and relevant conversations.
